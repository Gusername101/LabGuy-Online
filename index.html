Sample Inventory
Track every sample with custom metadata fields. Filter, search, move, and export with full audit trails on every change.
Drag & Drop Dashboard
Build your own workspace. Drag widgets onto the grid, resize them, and save your layout. Everyone gets their own personalised view.
Lab Structure & Capacity
Model your physical lab storage down to the deepest level. Monitor capacity in real time and see exactly what's stored where.
Role-Based Security
Three-tier role system with optional registration codes, login audit logs, and admin-controlled access.
Import & Export
Import samples from Excel or CSV. Export filtered inventory to Excel or PDF with custom column selection and live preview.
User Management
Manage your team's access, monitor who's online, review login history, and promote users — all from a single admin panel.
